Mango Sticky Rice

Description

Ever tried mango sticky rice before?

If you have a sweeet tooth, you'll love this Thai's mango sticky rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 ½ cups uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 1 ½ cups coconut milk, divided
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ¾ teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon tapioca starch
  • 3 mangos, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Get sticky rice
  2. Add some sauce made of coconut milk, sugar, salt & tapioca starch
  3. Put some mangoes on the top
